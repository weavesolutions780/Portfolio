About Me

A Decade of Trusted
Financial Practice

"Financial clarity isn't a luxury — it's the foundation every business deserves."

I am CA Rahul Sharma (FCA, DISA), a practising Chartered Accountant registered with the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI) since 2014. Over the past decade I have served a wide spectrum of clients — from individual taxpayers and HUFs to mid-size corporates and listed entities — across industries including manufacturing, real estate, healthcare, and fintech.

My practice is built on three pillars: technical accuracy, regulatory timeliness, and client-first communication. Every engagement is treated as a long-term relationship, not a one-time transaction.

Professional Journey

A Timeline of Growth

2010–14
CA Foundation, Intermediate & Final — ICAI
Institute of Chartered Accountants of India

Cleared all three levels of the CA examination in the first attempt. Completed mandatory articleship training under a Big-4 firm, gaining exposure to large-scale statutory audits, direct tax, and FEMA advisory.

2014–15
Associate — Audit & Assurance
National CA Firm, Mumbai

Handled statutory audits of listed companies in the FMCG and pharmaceuticals sector. Led a team of four for quarterly limited reviews and assisted in CARO reporting and Internal Financial Control assessments.

2015
Founded Sample & Associates
Independent Practice, Delhi NCR

Established an independent CA firm focusing on direct taxation, GST compliance, and SME advisory. Grew the client base from 12 to over 200 clients in the first three years through referrals and quality-first service.

2018
DISA Certification
ICAI — Diploma in Information Systems Audit

Qualified as a DISA to provide IT audit and systems advisory services. Began conducting information systems audits for NBFCs and co-operative banks, adding a technology dimension to the firm's service portfolio.

2020
Fellow Member of ICAI (FCA)

Elevated to Fellow status upon completing five years of active practice, recognising sustained contribution to the profession. Expanded services to include Transfer Pricing, FEMA compliance, and financial due diligence for M&A transactions.

2024+
