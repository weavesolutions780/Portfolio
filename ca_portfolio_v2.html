About Me

Practising CA with a
Decade of Experience

"Clarity in finance begins with clarity in communication."

I am CA Rahul Sharma (FCA, DISA), a Chartered Accountant in practice registered with the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI) since 2014. I hold a Fellow membership (FCA) and am also a qualified DISA from ICAI.

My practice, Sample & Associates, is based in Delhi NCR with an associate office in Mumbai. I work with individuals, partnership firms, LLPs, private limited companies, and non-profit organisations across a range of compliance and advisory engagements.

I believe in building long-term relationships with clients grounded in transparency, timeliness, and professional integrity.

Our Approach

How We Work With You

Clear Communication
